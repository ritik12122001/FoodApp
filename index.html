Precision Baking: AI for Accurate Ingredient Measurement

Baking is a science, yet most online recipes give measurements in cups and spoons—leading to unpredictable outcomes. A ‘cup of flour’ could be anywhere from 120g to 150g, completely changing the texture of your cake! Precision Baking solves this by using AI to convert any measurement into grams, ensuring perfect results every time.

Our team, Tech Titaans, has designed an innovative Android application that converts recipe measurements into precise grams. Additionally, it features AI-powered tools for direct user interaction through prompts. Our solution includes a comprehensive database of ingredient densities, real-time conversion algorithms, and a user-friendly interface, ensuring accurate and consistent results for all bakers.
